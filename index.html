AutoCAD software helps mechanical engineers excel in their jobs by helping them create preliminary designs and spot flaws before production, saving time and resources. It can provide a graphic simulation of the machine prototype and show it in action. This will enable engineers to make the necessary tweaks before getting into production. Mechanical engineers can also simulate various environments and pressure points on a prototype to test how the product will function in high-stress conditions.
